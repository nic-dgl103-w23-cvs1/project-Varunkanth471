Welcome to Sonsoles Print Studio

Studio Facilities

Sonsoles Print studio is a fully equipped studio, if you know how to screenprint but you are not familiar with our equipment, don't worry! That's what the induction is there for. Brand new equipment, Kippax Print Tables, x3 drying racks, screens and squeegees. Lockers, large working areas, A0 cutting mat and A0 plan chest for storage Very spacious, bright and high ceilings- insulated, heating and air con.
