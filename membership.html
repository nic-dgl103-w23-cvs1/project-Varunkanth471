Membership

A membership lasts one year and gives you access to the studio during our opening hours for print preparation. (opening hours may vary, please check with us before coming in). Printing sessions are paid for individually or in bulk as you wish and booked in advance (No monthly payments) More info below:

1 YEAR OPEN ACCESS MEMBERSHIP: £80

Open Access members must be competent working alone within the discipline of screen printing. But don't fear a technician will always be on site to give you advice and guidance with printing equipment and materials. Your membership will automatically start on the day of your induction. Membership includes 1 locker to store your materials for the year, access to shared plan chest storage, use of light box, cutting mats and preparation table, communal kitchen, eating area free WiFi. Please note we cannot keep members screens for long periods of time. A 1hr compulsory induction will then follow: £25 This will guide you through the Health Safety procedures and demonstrate how to prepare and expose a screen at SPS. It will also give you a thorough understanding of how our studio functions and provide you with the confidence needed to begin printing.
